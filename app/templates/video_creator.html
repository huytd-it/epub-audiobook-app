Thamso FFmpeg — Chi tiet

Audio Bitrate (-b:a)

Do bitrate quyet dinh chat luong va kich thuoc file audio trong video.

Gia triChat luongKich thuoc (1 phut)Nen dung khi
128kTrung binh~1 MBPodcast noi chuyen, audiobook giong doc don gian. Du nghe ro nhung dai tan cao (treb) bi cat bot.
192kTot (mac dinh)~1.4 MBPhu hop hau het truong hop. Can bang giua chat luong va kich thuoc.
256kRat tot~1.9 MBMusic podcast, audio co nhieu dai tan. Giu lai chi tiet tot hon.
320kCao nhat (MP3 max)~2.4 MBChat luong studio, music. File lon nhat nhung gan nhu nghe khac biet voi 256k tren loa thuong.
Luu y: FFmpeg encode audio sang AAC (-c:a aac) bat ke input. AAC hieu qua hon MP3 o cung bitrate. Bitrate 192k AAC tuong duong ~256k MP3 ve chat luong nghe. Neu file goc da la lossy (MP3, M4A), bitrate output khong nen cao hon bitrate goc — chi lam file lon hon khong them chat luong.

CRF — Constant Rate Factor (-crf)

Dieu chinh chat luong video (khong phai audio). CRF thap = chat luong cao = file lon.

CRFChat luongFile sizeUng dung
18Tot nhat (lossless-like)Rat lonArchive, master file
20Rat totLonChat luong cao, YouTube
23Tot (mac dinh)Vua phaiPhu hop nhat cho hau het use case
25Trung binhNho honGioi han bandwidth
28ThapNho nhatPreview, draft
Cach hoat dong: CRF la logarithmic — moi +6 CRF giam bitrate khoang 50%. Voi noi dung tinh (hinh anh + audio, khong phai video thuc), CRF 23 cho chat luong rat tot vi hinh anh khong thay doi nhieu.

Video Codec

CodecEngineUu diemNhuoc diem

Tune stillimage: Khi dung libx264, FFmpeg tu them -tune stillimage — toi uu encoder cho noi dung anh tinh + audio (giam bitrate khong can thiet cho motion estimation).

Image Type — Hieu ung hinh anh

Khi chon hieu ung (Ken Burns), FFmpeg dung zoompan filter de tao chuyen dong tu anh tinh:

TypeHieu ungChi tiet ky thuat
noneAnh tinhAnh duoc scale + pad giu nguyen aspect ratio, lap lai suot audio.
zoom-inPhong to danZoom tu 1.0x len 1.5x, giu trung tam. Tao cam quyet tap trung vao chi tiet.
zoom-outThu nho danZoom tu 1.5x xuong 1.0x. Mo ra tu chi tiet ra toan canh.
pan-leftTruot sang traiAnh zoom 1.2x, tuot tu phai sang trai. Tao cam quyet di chuyen.
pan-rightTruot sang phaiAnh zoom 1.2x, tuot tu trai sang phai.
Ky thuat: zoompan filter tinh toan so frame dua tren duration x fps. Vi du: audio 60s @ 30fps = 1800 frames, moi frame zoom them 0.0015x — tong zoom ~2.7x (bi clip o 1.5x). Anh input phai lon hon resolution output de co cho zoom/pan ma khong bi vo hinh.

Voi noi dung audiobook/podcast: 30fps la du vi hinh anh gan nhu tinh. 60fps chi co y nghia khi dung Ken Burns effect de chuyen dong muon hon — nhung cung dong nghia file lon hon 2x. Hinh anh duoc scale bang force_original_aspect_ratio=decrease + pad de vua resolution ma khong bi meo.

Dinh dang audio duoc ho tro

FormatCodecDac diemLuu y
.wavPCM (uncompressed)Chat luong goc, khong mat du lieuFile rat lon (~10MB/phut). Nen dung lam input.
.mp3MP3 (lossy)Pho bien nhat, tuong thuc moi noiBitrate goc quyet dinh chat luong toi da. Encode lai sang AAC co the mat them chat luong.
.m4aAAC (lossy)Hieu qua hon MP3, chat luong tot hon o cung bitrateFormat khuyen nghi cho audio lossy. Apple ecosystem dung format nay.
.oggVorbis (lossy)Ma nguon mo, chat luong totIt tuong thuc hon MP3/M4A tren mot so thiet bi.
Luong xu ly: Bat ke dinh dang input, FFmpeg luon encode output sang AAC (-c:a aac). Quy trinh: input codec decode → PCM → AAC encode → mux vao MP4. Vi vay, WAV (lossless) cho chat luong tot nhat lam input. Neu dung MP3/M4A, chon bitrate goc >= bitrate output de tranh generation loss.

FFmpeg command tuong duong

Moi video duoc tao tuong duong lenh:

ffmpeg -y -loop 1 -i image.jpg -i audio.mp3 \
  -c:v libx264 -tune stillimage \
  -vf "scale=1920:1080:force_original_aspect_ratio=decrease,pad=1920:1080:(ow-iw)/2:(oh-ih)/2" \
  -r 30 -c:a aac -b:a 192k \
  -pix_fmt yuv420p -crf 23 -shortest output.mp4
